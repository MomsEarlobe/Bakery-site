Our Story

Bread Made the Way
It Used to Be

No shortcuts. No preservatives. Just flour, water, salt, time — and hands that know what they're doing.

Small Batch, Always

Every loaf is shaped by hand in batches small enough to get right. We bake what we can do well — and nothing more.

Ingredients You Can Trust

Locally milled flour, real butter, free-range eggs. We keep it simple because the good stuff doesn't need help.

Baked Before Dawn

Our ovens fire up at 3 AM so everything is warm and ready when you walk through the door. That's just how we do it.

What We Bake

Honey Oat Wheat

$7.50

Soft whole wheat with a touch of local honey and rolled oats on top. A little sweet, a little nutty — the everyday loaf.

Rosemary Olive Oil

Fragrant rosemary and extra virgin olive oil baked into a tender, golden loaf. Incredible alongside soup or pasta.

Everything Ciabatta

Butter Croissant

$4.50

36 layers of laminated dough and real European butter. Flaky, golden, and shatteringly crisp. Our most popular pastry.

Almond Danish

Buttery danish filled with almond cream and topped with sliced almonds. Toasted golden and just sweet enough.

Blueberry Scone

Maple Pecan Loaf
